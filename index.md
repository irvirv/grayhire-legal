# GrayHire Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 12, 2026

GrayHire ("we", "us") is a website that allows users to sign in using third-party authentication providers (such as Facebook Login) to access site features.

## Information We Collect
When you sign in with Facebook, we may receive:
- Your name
- Email address
- Facebook user ID
- Profile picture (if provided)

We also collect basic technical information such as IP address, browser type, and timestamps in server logs.

## How We Use Information
We use this data to:
- Authenticate and manage your account
- Maintain login sessions
- Secure and operate the website

## Data Retention
We retain account information for as long as your account is active or as required to operate the service.

## Data Sharing
We do not sell personal data. Data is shared only as needed to operate the service or comply with legal obligations.

## Data Deletion
You may request deletion of your account by emailing:

**privacy@grayhire.com**

We will delete or anonymize your data after verifying the request.

## Contact
Questions about this policy can be sent to:

**privacy@grayhire.com**
